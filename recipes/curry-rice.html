Japanese Curry Rice

How to make simple Japanese curry rice

With this recipe you will learn how to make a simple and easy Japanese curry rice.

Before we get into the actual cooking, let's take a look at our list of ingredients first:

  • White, Short-Grain Rice, 2 cups
  • Curry Roux, 1 block
  • 1 Onion
  • 2 Large Potatoes
  • 2 Carrots
  • Chicken thighs, 250 gr
  • Salt
  • Soy Sauce
  • Seasoning Mix
  1. Slice the chicken into bite sized pieces, mix with seasoning and soy sauce, and leave it to marinade in the fridge
  2. Dice the onions
  3. Cut the carrots and potaotes into bite sized pieces
  4. Sautee the onions in a pot on medium high heat
  5. Add the chicken and cook until it changes color
  6. Add the potatoes and carrots and cook while mixing for couple of minutes
  7. Pour in 700ml of water and bring to a boil
  8. Once the water is boiling, remove the pot from the heat and add in the roux, mix well
  9. Put the pot back on heat and reduce it to low
  10. Cook until the potatoes break easily
  11. Cook the rice separately and serve it wth the curry
