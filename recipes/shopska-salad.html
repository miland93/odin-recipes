Shopska Salad

How to make Shopska Salad

With this recipe you will learn how to a refreshing cold salad, perfect for hot summer days!

For our ingredients we will need:

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumbers
  • Onion
  • Sirene Cheese
  • Vegetable Oil
  • Vinegar
  1. Wash and dice all the vegetable components
  2. Throw everything in a bowl
  3. Drizzle over some vegetable oil and vinegar, mix well
  4. Finally grate some sirene cheese over the top and serve!
