15 min Quesadilla

How to make a quick 15 min Quesadilla

With this recipe you will learn how to make quick and easy Quesadillas, that are cheap to boot!

First off, our ingredients list, each measure is per 1 quesadilla:

  • Tortilla
  • Baby Spinach, 125g
  • Bacon Strips/Bits 125g
  • Cheese Slices
  • Cream Cheese, 1 tablespoon
  • Tomato
  1. Cut bacon stripps into bits and start frying them on medium high heat in a pan
  2. Once you fry the bacon to your liking, thron in the baby spinach and cover the pan
  3. Wait until the spinach reduces in size and remove from the pan, keep the oil from the bacon
  4. Take a tortilla, spread the cream cheese on half of it
  5. Layer the spinach bacon mix over the spread, then the tomato slices, and finally the cheese slices on top
  6. Fold the tortilla in half, and repeat
  7. Finally throw the folded quesadillas back in the pan and fry on both sides until brown and crispy
  8. Cut the quesadilla into 3 triangle slices and serve!
